### 1. 线稿转写实图

**通用线稿转写实通用提示词模板**：

```
Strictly adhere to the input sketch's line art, proportions, and structure. No extraneous details. Generate a photorealistic jewelry render in 8K ultra-high resolution. Pure matte white background, shallow depth of field. Soft, even diffused lighting to prevent highlights from blowing out; preserve all metal and gemstone textures without overexposure. Achieve lifelike metal luster, accurate gemstone fire and refraction, crisp engraving layers, and precise setting details. Maintain absolute clarity and sharpness. Professional high-end jewelry visualization.

中文对照：
严格遵循输入草图的线条、比例和结构。不得添加任何无关细节。以8K超高分辨率生成逼真的珠宝渲染图。背景为纯哑光白色，景深较浅。采用柔和均匀的漫射光，以防止高光过曝；同时保留所有金属和宝石的纹理，避免过度曝光。实现逼真的金属光泽、精确的宝石火彩和折射效果、清晰的雕刻层次以及精准的镶嵌细节。保持绝对的清晰度和锐度。专业高端珠宝可视化效果。
```

### 2. AI  出 多视图

**珠宝手绘线稿转写实通用提示词**

```
Generate four standard perspectives based on the reference image (front (unchanged to match the reference image), left, right, and back (using orthogonal perspective to show only the rear structure of the work itself)) and present them in a 2x2 grid layout. The style, material, and craftsmanship details of the left, right, and back perspectives must match those of the reference image. All four views must come from a coherent 3D model. The left, right, and back perspectives must not include any decorations, distorted perspectives, or fictional structures. There must be no bases, supports, tripods, braces, pedestals, or balancing devices. It is prohibited to add supports behind or underneath them. Without considering gravity, each view must be geometrically faithful to the reference model. Soft, diffused studio lighting should be used, with priority given to ensuring clear details and avoiding overexposure and highlights. The background should be a pure white matte jewelry pad, with 8K high resolution and a professional jewelry photography quality

中文释义：
生成基于参考图的4个标准视角（正面（与参照图一致保持不变）、左侧、右侧、背面（采用正交透视法，仅展示作品本身的后部结构））并以2x2网格布局呈现，左侧、右侧、背面与参考图的风格、材质及工艺细节保持一致，所有四个视图必须来自一个连贯的三维模型。左侧、右侧、背面不得添加任何装饰、扭曲透视或虚构结构，无底座、无支架、无脚架、无支撑结构、无基座、无平衡装置。禁止在其背后或下方添加支撑，不用考虑重力，每个视图在几何上必须忠实于参考模型，柔和漫射影棚光线，优先保证细节清晰，避免过曝高光，纯白色哑光珠宝垫背景，8K高分辨率，专业珠宝摄影质感。
```

### 4. 图像立体化（灰度）

通用提示词模板：

```
Strictly adhere to the reference image: maintain exact 3D structure, proportions, perspective, and all sculptural details. No deviations in form, no added elements. Render as a pure clay model: single-tone matte gray material, no metal reflections, no gemstone refraction, no polishing. Use only subtle grayscale shading to convey form. Lighting: soft ambient occlusion (AO) only, minimal directional light, no harsh shadows, no specular highlights. Details must remain crisp—every texture, carving, and structural edge fully preserved. Dark matte black background, 8K resolution, clean topology-style presentation. No post-processing, no jewelry-style rendering, no photorealistic effects.


中文对照：
严格遵循参考图像：保持精确的3D结构、比例、透视以及所有雕塑细节。形式上不得有任何偏差，不得添加任何元素。渲染为纯黏土模型：单色调哑光灰色材质，无金属反射，无宝石折射，无抛光。仅使用微妙的灰度着色来传达形态。照明：仅使用柔和的环境光遮挡（AO），最小程度的平行光，无刺眼的阴影，无镜面高光。细节必须保持清晰——每个纹理、雕刻和结构边缘都要完全保留。背景为深哑光黑色，8K分辨率，拓扑风格简洁呈现。无后期处理，无珠宝风格渲染，无逼真效果。
```
